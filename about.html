Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Abhijeet Sapparapu, a dedicated and evolving full-stack web developer currently based in Hyderabad, India. I’m pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara, where I build projects blending technology with creativity and purpose.

My focus lies in web and backend development using JavaScript, React.js, Node.js/Express, and Django. I’m also exploring Django REST Framework, Generative AI, and API development.

I enjoy solving real-world problems through hackathons, personal experiments, and collaborative projects—from restaurant apps with live maps to hostel management and AI-based mood diaries.

While still early in my journey, I actively learn through debugging, building, and using AI Tools—not just for speed, but for deeper understanding.

I combine a solid CS foundation with a creative eye for design to build modern, user-friendly applications that make an impact.
